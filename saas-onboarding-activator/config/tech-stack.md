# Tech Stack — saas-onboarding-activator

## Analytics & Rastreamento Comportamental
- **Mixpanel** — Analytics de produto, funis, retenção e cohort analysis
- **Amplitude** — Product analytics, behavioral cohorts, retention curves
- **Segment** — Customer Data Platform (CDP) — coleta e roteamento de eventos
- **Heap** — Auto-capture de eventos, análise retroativa
- **PostHog** — Product analytics open-source, session recordings, feature flags

## Onboarding & In-App Messaging
- **Appcues** — Guided tours, tooltips, checklists, modals, hotspots
- **Pendo** — Product analytics + in-app guides + feedback
- **Intercom Product Tours** — Tours interativos integrados ao chat
- **Userpilot** — Onboarding contextual, NPS, feature adoption
- **Chameleon** — Tooltips, tours, launchers, microsurveys

## Email & Lifecycle Messaging
- **Customer.io** — Automação de emails baseada em comportamento
- **Iterable** — Cross-channel lifecycle marketing
- **Braze** — Customer engagement platform, push + email + in-app
- **Mailchimp** — Email marketing e automação básica

## Push Notifications
- **OneSignal** — Push notifications cross-platform
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging** — Push para iOS, Android, Web

## A/B Testing & Feature Flags
- **LaunchDarkly** — Feature flags e experimentação
- **Optimizely** — A/B testing e personalização
- **Split.io** — Feature flags e controlled rollout

## Product Management
- **Productboard** — Priorização de features e roadmap
- **Pendo Feedback** — Feedback de usuários integrado ao produto
